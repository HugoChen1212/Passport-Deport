Gathering required documents to Renew Your Passport

U.S. passport renewal application

DS-82 online

Click on "Submit" under "Complete Form Online" column, complete all required fields and print out the application. Be sure to sign and date your application. The DS-82 form must be completed online, applications without a barcode in the top left will not be accepted.

Letter of Authorization

Please download, print and sign 2 copies of the Letter of Authorization.

Two (2) passport photos

Photo must be taken within the past 6 months, 2x2 inches, and not from a vending machine.

Starting November 1, 2016, eye glasses will no longer be allowed in passport photos.

More on passport photo requirements.

Old (most recent) undamaged passport

Make sure that your old passport:

  • Was issued within the last 15 years.
  • You were at least 16 years of age at the time.

NOTE: Your previous passport will be returned to you with your new passport.

Travel itinerary

- Copy of a plane ticket to a foreign country, or

- Copy of a travel itinerary with the applicant's name showing dates and flight info, or if neither is available and traveling for business

- A letter from the employer.

For 2-3 business day passport service travel dates must be within 14 days when a visa is required to the country of destination; otherwise, dates must be within 5 days.

Government Fees

U.S. Department of State fee of $170.00 can be added to your order or please send a Certified Check or Money Order payable to the "U.S. Department of State" for $170.00

Shipping passport application for new passport to Passport Deport

Make sure following documents are included:

  1. The envelope sealed by the acceptance agent.
  2. Copy of a signed Letter of Authorization attached to the outside of the sealed envelope.

Please use delivery services the offer a tracking number such as FedEx, UPS or USPS Express.
