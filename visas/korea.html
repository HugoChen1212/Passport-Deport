    South Korea tourist visa is not required for citizens of United States of America for a stay up to 90 days.

    Fill out South Korea tourist visa application Form Download

    and send the following documents to Passport Deport

    • Visa Application Form
    • Valid Passport (must have at least 6 months of validity left and have at least 2 blank visa pages) and a copy of passport biographic page
    • 1 Passport Photo glued on Section # 1 (personal details), left side of the visa application form
    • Copy of airline ticket shows Entry and Departure of Korea
    • Copy of Bank Statement : Balance should be over $3,000
    • Copy of Hotel Information( or Address iin detail and Contact Phone Number in Korea)
    • Non US citizens must also submit a copy of : valid US permanent resident card or (a) valid U.S. visa (b) I-94 record (www.cbp.gov/i94) (c) I-20 or DS-2019 that has been recently signed forsigned for travel (F-1/2 and J-1/2 visa)

    South Korea business visa is not required for citizens of United States of America for a stay up to 90 days.

    Fill out South Korea business visa application Form Download

    and send the following documents to Passport Deport

    • Visa Application Form
    • Valid Passport (must have at least 6 months of validity left and have at least 2 blank visa pages) and a copy of passport biographic page
