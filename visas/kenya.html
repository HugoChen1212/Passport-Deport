    Kenya Visa application form. For online orders, please download, print and sign Kenya visa application, prepared by our system under your account.

    Original, signed United States of America passport with at least 6 months of remaining validity.

    Passport Photo: 2 Include a passport style photo, with a white background, taken within the last 6 months. You may also choose to upload a photo to your order for us to print. There is a surcharge associated with this service.

    Business Letter. business letter from either the sending company in the US or sponsoring company in Kenya. The letter should be printed on company letterhead stationery, addressed to the Embassy of Kenya, and signed by a senior manager (an equivalent to Vice-President or above). Business letters from US companies should adhere to the following guidelines:

    • Briefly introduce the applicant (please specify employment status/position held in the company by applicant).
    • State the nature of the business to be conducted (ie. business meetings, contract negotiations, etc.) and the names and addresses of companies to be contacted in Kenya.
    • HSpecify the Type and desired Validity of the visa (ie. one year multiple entry business visa).
    • Guarantee of sufficient funds for travel.
