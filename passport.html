Passport application processing through the local Post Office may take up to six months. PassportDepot.com passport experts submit passport applications directly to the U.S. Passport Agency and can process a new passport, replace lost passport or renew old passport in as little as 24 hours.

For rush 3-day passport orders please also call PassportDepot at 800-689-1388 to book your passport reservation slot. Applications received without an appointment will be handled on a case by case basis.

New passport

You are the first time applicant; Or your passport was issued more than 15 years ago;
Or your prior passport was issued at age 15 or under;
Or your current valid passport is damaged;
Or your expired passport is not in your possession;

passport Renewal

You already have a passport that is not damaged;
And you were over 16 at the time it was issued;
And your passport was issued within the last 15 years;
And you have not changed your legal name;
Or you canged your legal name and can prove it.

Passport for a child

You are the first time applicant; Or your passport was issued more than 15 years ago;
Or your prior passport was issued at age 15 or under;
Or your current valid passport is damaged;
Or your expired passport is not in your possession;

Passport name change

You changed your legal name since the passport was issued, and the passport's issue date is within one year, otherwise renew your passport.

Lost/Stolen Passport

Apply to replace your lost or stolen passport only if the missing passport is still valid. If the missing passport has expired, apply for a new passport.

Second Passport

Issued at the discretion of U.S Department of State to frequent travelers that do not have time to acquire visas between trips or for security reasons.
